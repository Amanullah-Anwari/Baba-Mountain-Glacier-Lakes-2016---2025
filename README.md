# Baba-Mountain-Glacier-Lakes-2016---2025
Change assessment of glacier lakes in Baba Mountains (Koh e Baba) between 2016 and 2025 based on Sentinel 2 Imageries
data used: Sentinel-2 Level-2A imagery (Google Earth Engine, COPERNICUS/S2_SR_HARMONIZED). Glacial lake polygon datasets (2016 and 2025). A picture of the glacial lake I took back in July 2023.
tools used: Python, Pandas, GeoPandas, Matplotlib, Google Earth Engine API, Geemap 
Summary: The analysis shows an increase from 35 lakes in 2016 to 40 lakes in 2025, with six newly formed lakes identified through spatial join analysis. Most lakes are located above 4000 meters, indicating elevation-dependent glacier retreat dynamics.
